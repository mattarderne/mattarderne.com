Winners of Future-Hack! - McKinsey Digital / Facebook

October 2017

This was a 3 day hackathon hosted by McKinsey Digital in Johannesburg, South Africa.

Our team built a recruitment tool based a JHipster backed and an Android app. The product placed students with companies to allow them to find job shadow opportunities and then follow on with job placements if the job shadow proves to be succesful.
